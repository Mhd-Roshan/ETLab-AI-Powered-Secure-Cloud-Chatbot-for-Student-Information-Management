## KTU (APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University)

### About KTU
- Full Name: APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University
- Established: 2015
- Location: Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala
- Type: State Technical University
- Jurisdiction: All engineering colleges in Kerala

### Academic Structure
- **System**: Choice Based Credit System (CBCS)
- **Semesters**: 8 semesters for B.Tech (4 years)
- **Academic Year**: July to June
- **Semester Duration**: Approximately 6 months each

### KTU Syllabus Structure
1. **Theory Courses**: 3-4 credits per course
2. **Lab Courses**: 1-2 credits per course
3. **Projects**: Final year project (6-8 credits)
4. **Electives**: Open electives and professional electives
5. **Mandatory Courses**: 
   - Engineering Mathematics
   - Professional Communication
   - Life Skills
   - Humanities courses

### Evaluation System
- **Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE)**: 50 marks
  - Assignments
  - Tests (2-3 per semester)
  - Attendance
  - Seminar/Viva
- **Semester End Examination (SEE)**: 50 marks
  - University conducted exams
  - 3-hour duration
  - Theory and problem-solving

### Grading System (10-point CGPA)
- **S**: 10 (Outstanding - 90-100%)
- **A+**: 9 (Excellent - 85-89%)
- **A**: 8.5 (Very Good - 80-84%)
- **B+**: 8 (Good - 75-79%)
- **B**: 7 (Above Average - 70-74%)
- **C**: 6 (Average - 60-69%)
- **P**: 5 (Pass - 50-59%)
- **F**: 0 (Fail - Below 50%)

### Attendance Requirements
- **Minimum Attendance**: 75% mandatory
- **Condonation**: Up to 10% with valid medical certificates
- **Below 65%**: Not eligible for university exams
- **Calculation**: Includes theory and lab classes

### Examination Regulations
1. Students must have 75% attendance to appear for exams
2. Two examination chances per year (Regular + Supplementary)
3. Improvement exams allowed for passed subjects
4. Maximum duration to complete degree: 12 years
5. Minimum credits required: 160 credits for B.Tech
